My top 3 movies of all time

Silent Voice

This movie is a correct representation of emotions dealing with stress, depression, social anxiety and a movie showcasing a great romance.

Shutter Island

This is the best thriller movie that i have watched ever and probably has the most confusing ending to it.

Wailing

A South Korean thriller which is more horrifying than any of those cliche american horror movies out there and has the most scariest endings out there.
